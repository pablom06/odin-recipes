Chicken Piccata

Description
This is my world famous chicken piccata recipe. It has been made on several continents.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Capers
  • 1 Cup of Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cayenne
  • Fresh Lemon
  • 1 Stick of Butter
  • 1 cup of White Cooking Wine
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Asiago cheese
  • 1 Cup of Chicken stock
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Trim chicken breast.
  2. Pound chicken until tender and flat.
  3. Add salt, pepper and light cayenne to both sides of the breasts.
  4. Put flour, salt, pepper and parmesean cheese in bowl together.
  5. Coat chicken in the flour mixture.
  6. Cook chicken in butter in sautee pan.
  7. Leave chicken remains/greese in the pan.
  8. While still hot add capers with butter and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Add white wine, chicken stock and lemon slices.
  10. Cook wine until it cooks out. Usually about 10-15 minutes. Wine should thicken and lose bitter taste.
  11. Add parmesean cheese, asiago cheese for flavor and to thicken and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  12. Taste and if need more cheese add. If too salty add water. If too lemony add cheese and water.
  13. Add flour to thicken sauce to likening and add chicken to sauce and let sit on low heat for 5 minutes
  14. Add parsley for garnishing
