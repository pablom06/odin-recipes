Penne alla Vodka

Description
This is my Penne alla vodka recipe that I have been told is better than grandma from Italy (but don't tell her).

Ingredients
  • Penne
  • Vodka
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Canned San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Asiago cheese
  • 1 Cup of Chicken stock
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Bacon
  • Heavy Cream
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil and Oregano

Steps
  1. Bring a pot of water to boil to cook the pasta.
  2. Sautee bacon or prosciutto. Heat oil in saucepan, then add onion and bacon or prosciutto. Sautee for 4-5 minutes, add garlic and red pepper flakes.
  3. Remove pan from heat and add tomatoes, vodka, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Add some Parmesean and Asiago cheese to flavor and bring back to a medium heat to simmer. Let simmer until alcohol is cooked off (no alcohol smell). Stir occassionally.
  4. Cook pasta in water al dente (little hard) and drain.
  5. Stir cream into sauce and let cook through for about 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add in parmesean, basil, oregano.
  7. Take pasta and toss in with completed sauce.
  8. Serve pasta on plate garnished with mozzarella cheese and basil.
