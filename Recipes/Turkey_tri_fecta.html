Turkey Tri Fecta

Description
This is my famous turkey that people who don't eat turkey eat. I grew up in a house where the turkey was so dry that it crumbled. I was determined to grow up and make the perfect, fall off the bone turkey.

Ingredients
  • Turkey
  • Chicken Stock
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chicken Bouillion
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Adobo
  • Aluminum Foil
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • White Cooking Wine

    Steps
  1. Clean out turkey and remove giblets.
  2. Make a mixture of butter, white cooking wine, chicken stock, adobo and bouillion.
  3. Cut potatoes, celery and carrots. Stuff turkey with them.
  4. Inject turkey with butter mixture. Make sure injected all over.
  5. Cover outside of turkey with bouillion.
  6. Add salt, pepper and some olive oil to outside of turkey.
  7. Put wine and chicken stock in pan under turkey
  8. Take aluminum foil and tent the turkey.
  9. Cook on 450 for about 6-7 hours.
  10. Last hour take off tenting and baste and inject juice from turkey.
